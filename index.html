Café Dataset Update – Vienna 1st District

Visualizing air conditioning availability in cafés to support heat adaptation in urban spaces

Motivation

Everyone knows the situation: it’s over 30 °C, you’re strolling through the city and decide to take a break in a café to cool down – only to realise it’s just as hot inside.

The inner district of Vienna is densely built and heats up in summer. Cool indoor locations such as cafés can be important for vulnerable groups like older adults. However, it’s unclear which cafés offer air conditioning at all.

OpenStreetMap includes an air_conditioning tag, but it’s rarely used. In our initial dataset of 139 cafés, only 6 had AC info. This lack of reliable data motivated our project.

Initial Dataset (Before)

  • 139 cafés in Vienna’s 1st district
  • Only 6 had air conditioning data (~4%)
  • Missing information in key fields:
    • 26% no opening_hours
    • 32% no street name
    • 53% no website

Data Update and Cleaning

We reviewed each entry manually, validated data via web search and local knowledge, and added missing information or deleted outdated records.

  • Cleaned dataset includes 120 cafés
  • 69 cafés have air conditioning (~58%)
  • 43 do not (~36%)
  • 8 still have no information (~7%)
